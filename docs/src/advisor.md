# Morning advisory

The sentence at the top of the Irrigation tab that explains today in
plain words: what's running, what's skipping, and the one reason that
matters.

It's assembled from the engine's actual decision (never a guess), and
when the optional AI advisor is configured it gets a more natural
voice; without one, a deterministic template produces the same facts.

The advisory updates whenever the decision does: forecast refreshes,
threshold changes, manual runs, and probe readings can all change
tonight's plan, and the sentence follows.
